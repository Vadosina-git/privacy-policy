Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Calm Wave ("we", "our", "the app") is a voice journaling application designed for stress relief and personal well-being. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

The app collects the following types of data:

  • Audio recordings — voice journal entries you create within the app. These are stored locally on your device.
  • Mood data — mood selections you make after each recording, stored locally on your device.
  • App usage data — basic analytics such as session duration, feature usage, and crash reports to help us improve the app.

2. Microphone Access

The app requires access to your device's microphone to record voice journal entries. Microphone access is used solely for this purpose. We do not record audio in the background or without your explicit action (pressing the record button).

3. Data Storage

All personal data, including audio recordings and mood entries, is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your recordings to external servers unless you explicitly opt in to AI-powered features (see below).

4. AI-Powered Features (Optional)

The app may offer optional AI-powered features, such as transcription and personalized insights. If you choose to use these features:

  • Your audio may be processed by third-party AI services (such as OpenAI) to generate transcriptions and insights.
  • We will always ask for your explicit consent before processing any audio through third-party services.
  • Processed data is not stored by third-party services beyond what is necessary to complete the request.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

The app offers premium subscription plans. All payments are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information directly.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — for subscription management. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI — for optional AI features (transcription and insights). See OpenAI Privacy Policy.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with third-party services as described above, and only with your explicit consent.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete any recording at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data. If you have used AI features, you can request deletion of any processed data by contacting us.

9. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app or on our website.
