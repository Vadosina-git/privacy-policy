Frequently Asked Questions

What is this app?

Video Poker Vegas Classic 3D is a practice and study app for the five-card draw card game, featuring 10 classic video poker variants. It's designed for entertainment, learning, and strategy practice only. No real money is involved at any point.

Can I win real money?

No. All credits in this app are virtual and have no real-world value. They cannot be purchased, exchanged, redeemed, transferred, or cashed out. This app does not offer any real-money rewards or prizes.

How do I get more practice credits?

Virtual credits refill automatically through a free daily bonus and hourly gift system. You never need to wait long or pay anything to continue practicing.

How do I improve my video poker strategy?

Each variant in the app shows its full paytable on every hand. Start with Jacks or Better to learn the fundamentals, then explore other variants to understand how different paytables affect optimal strategy. Multi Hand mode is useful for seeing how variance plays out across many hands of the same decision.

Lost my progress?

Progress is saved locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will reset your progress. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your history.

The app isn't working

Please make sure you're running iOS 15 or later. If the problem persists, contact alzeydi@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version.
