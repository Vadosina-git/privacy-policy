Frequently Asked Questions

What is this app?

Video Poker Vegas Classic 3D is a card game simulation featuring 10 classic video poker variants. It's designed for entertainment and strategy practice only. No real money is involved at any point.

Can I win real money?

No. All credits in this app are virtual and have no real-world value. They cannot be exchanged, redeemed, or cashed out.

My credits are low — how do I get more?

Virtual credits refill automatically via a free daily bonus and hourly gift system. You never need to wait long or pay anything to continue playing.

Lost my progress?

Progress is saved locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will reset your progress. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your history.

The game isn't working

Please make sure you're running iOS 15 or later. If the problem persists, contact support@khralz.com with your device model and iOS version.
