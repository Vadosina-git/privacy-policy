Frequently Asked Questions

What is this app?

Drop Seven: Numbers merge is a single-player number puzzle played on a 7x7 board. You drop numbered discs, and a disc clears when its number equals how many discs are in its row or column. It is for entertainment only — there is no real-money gambling of any kind.

How do I play?

Tap a column to drop the current disc. A disc disappears when its number matches the count of discs in its row or column. Cleared discs can trigger chain reactions for bonus points. Crack the grey blanks to reveal hidden numbers. The round ends when the board overflows.

What are lives and ads?

Lives let you start rounds and refill automatically over time. You can optionally watch a rewarded ad to continue a round. Ads are provided by Google AdMob. The app shows an App Tracking Transparency prompt to ask for your choice; you can decline and still play normally.

Lost my progress?

Your scores and progress are saved locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will reset them. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your records.

The app isn't working

Please make sure you're running iOS 15 or later. If the problem persists, contact vakhrustalev@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version.
