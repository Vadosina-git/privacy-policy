Frequently Asked Questions

What is this app?

Will it Crush? is a casual idle game. Objects fall between spinning rollers and get crushed into coins. You spend coins on upgrades (speed, power, gravity, teeth and more) to crush bigger objects and earn faster. It is for entertainment only — there is no real-money gambling of any kind.

How do I play?

Objects fall automatically and the rollers crush them. Tap objects to help crush them, tap the rollers to temporarily double their speed, and sell rare minerals for big payouts. Buy upgrades from the bottom panel, complete daily tasks, collect puzzle currency to unlock roller skins, and trigger the bonus mode when it charges up.

What are gems, coins and puzzles?

Coins are the main currency earned by crushing. Gems are a premium currency used for permanent income multipliers; you earn some in-game and can optionally buy more. Puzzle pieces are earned from rare minerals and tasks and unlock roller skins.

What about ads?

The app shows ads provided by Google AdMob: a banner, occasional full-screen ads, and optional rewarded ads you can choose to watch for a bonus. On first launch we show a consent / App Tracking Transparency prompt; you can decline and still play normally. A one-time "Remove Ads" purchase is available.

Lost my progress?

Your progress is saved locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will reset it. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your records.

The app isn't working

Please make sure you're running a recent version of iOS or Android. If the problem persists, contact vakhrustalev@gmail.com with your device model and OS version.
