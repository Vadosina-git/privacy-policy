Privacy Policy — Veggie Rush - Merge Mania

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Veggie Rush - Merge Mania ("we", "our", "the app"), developed and managed by KHRALZ (package name com.khralz.fruitmerge), is a casual puzzle game where players merge fruits to reach the watermelon tier. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

The app collects the following types of data:

  • Game progress — best scores, current game state, tutorial completion, language and audio preferences. Stored locally on your device.
  • In-app purchase data — non-consumable and consumable purchase receipts processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. The app does not store payment information directly.
  • Diagnostic data — anonymous crash reports and aggregated usage metrics provided by the platform (App Store / Google Play) to help us improve the app.

2. Data Storage

All game data (scores, settings, saved sessions, life counters) is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your game progress to external servers.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

The app offers in-app purchases (extra lives packs and unlimited-lives unlock). All payments are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information directly.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play — for app distribution, in-app purchases and anonymous diagnostic data.
  • RevenueCat (planned) — for subscription and purchase management. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with third-party services as described above.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Game data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data, including scores and saved sessions.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is rated for general audiences but is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Advertising

The app does not currently show third-party advertisements. If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated and your consent will be requested where required by law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app or on this page.
