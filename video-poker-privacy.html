We collect a minimal amount of technical data to operate the App. Specifically:

We do not collect: your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, contacts, photos, files, location, microphone, camera, or advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID).

All gameplay progress (chip balance, settings, statistics, daily-quest progress, claim cooldowns) is stored locally on your device in the App's private storage area. This data:
