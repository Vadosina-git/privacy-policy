Privacy Policy — Bubble Words

Last updated: June 2026

Short version: Bubble Words does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game runs fully offline, shows no ads, uses no analytics, and requires no account.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal data. Bubble Words does not ask you to sign up, log in, or provide a name, email address, or any other personal detail. We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics tools, or any third-party services that would gather information about you or your device.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Your game progress (completed levels, coins, and settings) is saved locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or anyone else, and is removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Internet and Third Parties

Bubble Words works fully offline and does not require an internet connection to play. We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any.

4. Children's Privacy

Bubble Words is suitable for all ages. Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none from children either. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

5. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

6. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at alzeydi@gmail.com. We respond within 48 hours.
