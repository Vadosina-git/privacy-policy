Frequently Asked Questions

What is Veggie Rush?

Veggie Rush is a single-player merge puzzle. You drop fruit into a jar — when two of the same kind touch, they merge into the next, bigger fruit. Keep combining all the way up to the watermelon, the biggest tier in the game.

How do I play?

Tap to aim and release to drop the current fruit into the jar. When two identical fruit collide, they merge into a larger one and score points. Plan your drops so the jar doesn't overfill — if the stack reaches the top line for too long, the game ends.

Is my progress saved?

Yes. Your current game and best score are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will reset your progress, since nothing is stored on our servers.

What are the power-ups for?

Power-ups help you out of tricky spots — for example removing a single fruit, clearing a small area, upgrading a fruit to the next tier, or reshuffling the jar. You start with a few and can get more in the in-app shop.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?

The app may show ads and offers optional in-app purchases. All purchases are processed securely by Apple or Google. Virtual items have no real-world monetary value and cannot be cashed out.

The app isn't working

Please make sure your device is running a recent OS version (iOS 15 or later). If the problem continues, email alzeydi@gmail.com with your device model and OS version, and we'll help you out.
