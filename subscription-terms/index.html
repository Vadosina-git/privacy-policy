Prices may vary by region and are displayed in your local currency at the time of purchase.

2. Free Trial

The yearly plan includes a 3-day free trial. During the trial period, you have full access to all premium features at no charge. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid subscription.

3. Premium Features

A premium subscription unlocks:

  • Unlimited daily voice recordings (free users are limited to 2 per day)
  • Access to all custom themes (Elegant, Light, and Dark collections)

4. Auto-Renewal

All subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.

5. Managing Your Subscription

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Calm Wave
  • Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Calm Wave

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You will retain access to premium features until that date.

6. Payment

All payments are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Calm Wave does not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. Payment terms are subject to the policies of your app store.

7. Refunds

Refund requests are handled by the respective app store:

  • Apple: Request a refund from Apple
  • Google: Request a refund from Google Play

8. Changes to Subscription Terms

We may update subscription plans, pricing, or these terms from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through the app or our website prior to taking effect. Continued use of premium features after changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
