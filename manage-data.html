Manage Your Data

Last updated: March 26, 2026

At Calm Wave, you are in full control of your personal data. This page explains what data we collect, how you can manage it, and how to request its deletion.

1. Data We Store

Depending on the features you use, Calm Wave may store the following data:

  • Account information — email address and display name associated with your account.
  • Voice recordings — audio journal entries you create within the app.
  • Mood history — mood selections you make after each recording.
  • App preferences — your settings, language, and notification preferences.
  • Analytics data — anonymous usage data such as session duration, feature usage, and crash reports to help us improve the app.

2. Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In the app: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  • By email: Send a request to alzeydi@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account.
Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your recordings, mood history, and personal data will be permanently erased from our servers within 30 days.

3. Delete Individual Recordings

You can delete any individual voice recording at any time directly in the app. Swipe left on a recording or tap the delete button to remove it. Deleted recordings are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

4. Opt Out of Analytics

You can disable analytics data collection at any time:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy → disable "Share Analytics Data".

When analytics are disabled, we will no longer collect usage data or crash reports from your device. This does not affect app functionality.

5. Manage Notifications

You can manage or disable all notifications from Calm Wave:

  • In the app: Settings → Notifications.
  • On your device: iOS Settings → Calm Wave → Notifications.

6. Export Your Data

You have the right to request a copy of all your personal data. To do so, please contact us at alzeydi@gmail.com. We will provide your data in a portable format within 30 days of your request.

7. Cancel Subscription

Your subscription is managed through the Apple App Store. To cancel:

  • Open iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Calm Wave → Cancel.

Cancelling a subscription does not delete your account or data. You will retain access to premium features until the end of your current billing period.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR and CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restriction — request we limit processing of your data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format.
  • Objection — object to processing of your data for certain purposes.

9. Contact Us

For any data-related requests or questions, please contact us:

Email: alzeydi@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all requests within 30 days.
