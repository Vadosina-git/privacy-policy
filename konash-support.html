Frequently Asked Questions

What is this app?

Go Home Konash is a single-player, one-button top-down arcade game. You control a knight whose blade spins around him and cuts down enemies on contact as you climb your way home through waves of skeletons. It is for entertainment only — there is no real-money gambling of any kind.

How do I play?

Your blade spins on its own and damages any enemy it touches. Tap the screen to perform a short dash in the direction your blade is pointing. Hold to stop spinning and charge a powerful lunge, then release to strike. Collect coins from defeated enemies, level up, and upgrade your weapon at the blacksmith. Reach the top of the biome and beat the boss.

What are lives and ads?

You have a fixed number of hearts per run; getting hit costs a heart. You can optionally watch a rewarded ad for bonuses, and the app may show banner and interstitial ads. Ads are provided by Google AdMob. The app shows an App Tracking Transparency prompt to ask for your choice; you can decline and still play normally. Optional in-app purchases let you remove ads or buy coins.

Lost my progress?

Your high scores and progress are saved locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will reset them. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your records.

The app isn't working

Please make sure you're running a supported OS version. If the problem persists, contact vakhrustalev@gmail.com with your device model and OS version.
