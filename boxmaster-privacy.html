Privacy Policy

Boxmaster — 3D Sokoban Puzzle

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Summary: Boxmaster is a single-player 3D puzzle game. All progress is stored locally on your device. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, advertising ID, or any personal information. The app does not contain ads or third-party tracking.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Boxmaster ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our mobile application available on iOS and Android.

The App is developed and published by Khralz ("the Developer").

2. Information We Collect

Boxmaster is a single-player offline puzzle game. We collect no personal data of any kind. Specifically:

  • Local game progress — completed levels, current level, move counts, hint balance, settings (sound on/off, control scheme, character selection). Stored on your device only; never transmitted to us or anyone else.

We do not collect: your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, contacts, photos, files, location, microphone, camera, or advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID). The App does not contact any server during normal gameplay.

3. Local Game Data

All gameplay progress (completed levels, hint balance, settings, character selection) is stored locally on your device in the App's private storage area. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not associated with your identity
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App
  • Can be reset at any time using the in-App "Reset Progress" option in Settings

4. Hints (Virtual Currency)

The App uses hints as a virtual gameplay resource that regenerates over time. Hints have no real-world monetary value, cannot be cashed out, traded, or exchanged for real money or anything of value. There is no gambling of any kind in the App.

5. In-App Purchases

The current version of the App does not offer in-app purchases. If in-app purchases are added in the future, they will be processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We would not receive, store, or process any payment information (card number, billing address, etc.). This Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly before any such feature is enabled.

6. Notifications

The current version of the App does not send push notifications. If notification features are added in the future (for example, to remind you that hints have refilled), they will be scheduled and delivered entirely on your device by your operating system, with no personal content, and you will be asked for explicit permission first.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and contains no gambling, ads, or user-to-user communication. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children. The App is configured so that its traffic (if any is added in the future) would not be treated as targeted to children under applicable laws.

8. Third-Party Services

The current version of the App does not integrate any third-party services. Specifically: no Google Analytics, no Firebase, no Facebook SDK, no advertising networks, no AppsFlyer, no Adjust, no attribution or behavioral-tracking SDK of any kind.

If third-party services are integrated in future versions (for example, Firebase Remote Config for balance tuning, or in-app billing through Apple / Google), this Privacy Policy will be updated to disclose them before the corresponding version ships.

9. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Vibration (Android) — for haptic feedback when pushing boxes or completing levels. Can be disabled in App Settings.

The App does not request access to your microphone, camera, location, contacts, photos, files, or network state.

10. Data Retention

Local game data persists on your device until you delete the App or reset progress in Settings. We do not retain any data on our own servers because we do not collect any.

11. Data Security

Local game data is protected by your device's operating-system sandbox. Since we do not collect or store personal data on any server, the data we hold cannot be breached on our side.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights under privacy laws such as the GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA (California), and similar regulations — including the right to access, delete, and port your personal data. Because Boxmaster collects no personal data and stores all gameplay data locally on your device, you can exercise these rights directly: deleting the App or resetting progress in Settings will remove all data associated with your use of the App.

13. International Transfers

No data is transferred internationally because no data is transmitted off your device.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, particularly if features that involve data collection (such as in-app purchases or remote configuration) are added. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We recommend reviewing this page periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
