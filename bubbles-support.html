Frequently Asked Questions

What is this app?

Bubble Words is a relaxing word puzzle. Each level fills the board with floating word bubbles, and your job is to sort every word into the basket where it belongs. Match a whole category and the bubbles merge and pop. Clear the board to win.

How do I play?

Drag each word bubble into the category basket it belongs to. When a category is complete, its bubbles merge and pop. A level is solved once the board is cleared. Take your time — there are no timers.

I'm stuck on a level. Can I get a hint?

Yes. Tap the Hint button to highlight a word and where it should go. Use it whenever you need a nudge.

Does the app show ads or need an account?

No. Bubble Words has no ads, no sign-up, and no account. It works fully offline.

Lost my progress?

Progress is saved locally on your device. Uninstalling the app resets your progress, so we recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your completed levels.

The game isn't working

Please make sure you're running a supported OS version (iOS 13 or later). If the problem persists, contact alzeydi@gmail.com with your device model and OS version.
